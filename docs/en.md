# Sweet Spot - Complete User Guide

Welcome to the Sweet Spot Customer Intelligence platform! This guide will explain how to use every feature on the site to get the most value out of your customer data.

## 1. Dashboard
The Dashboard gives you a complete overview of your customer base.
- **Metrics Grid**: Shows your Total Customers, Average Score, % of Top Customers, and Total Revenue.
- **Profitability vs Effort Graph**: A quadrant chart plotting your customers based on the effort they require versus the profit they generate. The top-left quadrant represents the "Optimal Growth Zone" (high profit, low effort). The "Top" customers will pulse on the graph automatically.
- **Industry Breakdown**: Displays the distribution of your customers across different industries in a bar chart.
- **Top 10 Customers**: A list of your highest-scoring customers based on the criteria weights you've set in the Settings.

## 2. Customers (Raw Data)
This is where you manage your client roster. 
- **Add Customer**: Click this button to add a new client. You will fill out their basic information (Name, Industry) and objective metrics (Revenue, Profit Margin in EUR, Effort in hours).
- **Subjective Evaluation**: When adding or editing a customer, you also evaluate them on subjective traits:
  - Chemistry Score (1-5)
  - Growth Potential (1-5)
  - Payment Willingness (1-5)
  - Repeat Rate (%)
  - Recommendations (Count)
These metrics are combined by our engine to calculate the customer's overall "Sweet Spot" score.

## 3. Settings (Scoring Engine Weights)
This is the core of the Sweet Spot engine. 
- You can assign specific percentage weights (from 0% to 100%) to 7 different criteria: Profitability, Effort, Chemistry, Growth Potential, Payment Willingness, Repeat Rate, and Recommendations.
- **Important**: The total weight must always equal exactly 100%.
- Once you click "Save Weights", the system will recalculate the score and rank for every single customer based on your new preferences. This directly updates the Dashboard's "Top 10" list.

## 4. Team & Roles
Manage who has access to your workspace.
- **Invite Members**: Send email invitations for colleagues to join your workspace.
- **Roles**: Assign either an "Admin" or "User" role. Admins can manage other users, delete records, and change system settings, while standard Users have restricted access. The original account creator is permanently protected as an Admin.

## 5. Multi-Language Support
Sweet Spot is available in 10 languages (English, German, Spanish, French, Chinese, Arabic, Hindi, Portuguese, Russian, and Japanese). You can change your language preference from the top navigation bar or footer to instantly translate all interface elements.
